Overview

About PocketPal

PocketPal is a personal budgeting and expense-tracking app built by Bewandera Labs, a small independent team based in Nairobi, Kenya.

PocketPal helps you:

  • Track income and everyday expenses
  • Create and manage budgets
  • Monitor savings goals
  • See simple insights into your spending habits
  • Securely back up your financial records to your own Google Drive — entirely optional
Data collection

What we collect

A short, honest list of what PocketPal sees — and what it deliberately never touches.

Information you choose to share

Google account

When you choose to sign in with Google, PocketPal uses your account only to authenticate you and connect your encrypted backup to your own Drive. We may access your email address, display name, and profile photo (if you have one).

We never access your emails, contacts, calendar, or any other part of your Google account.

Financial data

The budgets, expenses, income, savings goals, notes, and reminders you enter remain yours. PocketPal only uses this information to run the features you're actually using in the app.

Your security passphrase

When you turn on encrypted backups, you create a personal passphrase. It protects your backup and is never stored anywhere in a form that lets us — or anyone else — read it.

Because only you know it, we genuinely cannot recover it if it's forgotten. More on that below.

Collected automatically

Device information

A device ID (used to identify your backup), your operating system version, and the app version you're running.

Usage & crash data

Anonymized crash reports and performance metrics via Firebase Crashlytics, so we can catch and fix bugs.

Advertising ID

An anonymous advertising identifier, used only to show ads if you're on the free tier. PocketPal Premium removes this entirely.

What PocketPal never collects

No location tracking, no contacts, no hidden access. Full stop.

Usage & security

How we use — and protect — your information

How we use it

  • Authentication — verifying it's really you via Google Sign-In
  • Cloud backup — securely storing your encrypted data in your own Google Drive
  • App functionality — powering budgeting, expense tracking, reminders, and insights
  • Crash reporting — catching and fixing bugs from anonymized data
  • Advertising — showing relevant ads, only for free-tier users

Security, built in from day one

Your financial information is encrypted before it ever leaves your device. Even if someone somehow accessed your cloud backup, all they'd find is unreadable, encrypted data — never your actual budgets or transactions.

PocketPal uses industry-standard cryptography to make that true:

  • Your passphrase generates an encryption key using PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations
  • Your data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before upload
  • On-device data is stored using encrypted local storage (Hive, AES-256)
  • We — the developers — have no way to decrypt your data. Only your passphrase can

Please keep your passphrase somewhere safe. Because it's the only key to your data, there's no "forgot password" recovery option — by design, so no one but you can ever unlock your backup.

Storage

Where your data actually lives

Storage locations

  • On your device — encrypted local storage (Hive, AES-256)
  • Google Drive — encrypted backups sit in your account's private appDataFolder, a hidden space only PocketPal and you can reach. No other app, and no other person, can see it
  • Firebase — used for authentication (email/user ID only) and anonymized crash reporting

How long we keep it

  • Your data stays on your device and in Drive until you delete it
  • Uninstalling without a backup permanently deletes your local data
  • You can remove your Drive backup any time from your Google Drive settings

Control

Sharing, disclosure & your rights

We respect your privacy

PocketPal does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information — ever. We share information only when it's necessary to:

  • Comply with the law, a court order, or a government regulation
  • Operate the third-party services listed above, and only to the extent needed
  • Complete a business transfer, such as an acquisition or merger — you'll always be notified first

Your rights & choices

  • Access — view all your data directly inside the app
  • Deletion — uninstall or use "Clear Data" in Settings for local data; remove hidden app data from Google Drive → Settings → Manage Apps for your cloud backup; sign out any time to end your session
  • Portability — export your data as a JSON file from Settings, whenever you like
  • Opt out of ads — PocketPal Premium removes ads entirely
  • Revoke permissions — manage notifications, alarms, and other permissions from your device settings
Legal

The fine print

Children's privacy

PocketPal isn't intended for anyone under 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has shared information with us, please reach out right away and we'll act promptly.

International data transfers

Because Google Drive and Firebase run on global infrastructure, your encrypted data may be stored outside Kenya. By using PocketPal, you agree to this transfer. We only work with services that meet recognized data-protection standards, including GDPR and CCPA where they apply.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll let you know by posting the update in the app, updating the date at the top of this page, and — for anything significant — sending an in-app notification. Continuing to use PocketPal after a change means you accept the update.

Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of Kenya, and any disputes are resolved under Kenyan jurisdiction.
