2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information (name, profile picture) for authentication purposes only.
  • Financial Data: Budget information, transactions, income, expenses, notes, reminders, and goals that you manually enter into the app.
  • Security Passphrase: You create a passphrase to encrypt your data. We do not store or have access to your passphrase - it never leaves your device in plain text.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device ID (for identifying your backup), operating system version, app version.
  • Usage Data: App crashes, performance metrics (via Firebase Crashlytics - anonymized).
  • Advertising ID: Anonymous advertising identifier for showing ads (if you haven't purchased Premium).

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts or phone numbers
  • ❌ Photos or camera access (except for receipt scanning feature - processed locally)
  • ❌ SMS or call logs
  • ❌ Microphone or audio

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity via Google Sign-In.
  • Cloud Backup: To securely store your encrypted data in your personal Google Drive (appDataFolder - only accessible by you and Pocket Pal).
  • App Functionality: To provide budgeting, expense tracking, reminders, and analytics features.
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix bugs (anonymized data via Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads (only for free tier users via Google AdMob).

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Encryption

Your financial data is protected with bank-grade, end-to-end encryption (AES-256-GCM).

  • Your passphrase is used to generate an encryption key (KEK) via PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations.
  • Data is encrypted on your device before being uploaded to Google Drive.
  • We (the developers) cannot decrypt your data - only you can with your passphrase.
  • ⚠️ Important: If you forget your passphrase, we cannot recover your data. There is no "forgot password" option for security reasons.

4.2 Storage Locations

  • Local Storage: Your data is stored on your device using encrypted storage (Hive with AES-256).
  • Google Drive: Encrypted backups are stored in your personal Google Drive's "appDataFolder" - a special folder only accessible by Pocket Pal and you. Other apps and users cannot access this folder.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase Authentication (email/user ID only) and Crashlytics (anonymized crash reports).

4.3 Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device and Google Drive until you delete it.
  • If you uninstall the app without backup, local data is permanently deleted.
  • You can delete your Google Drive backup anytime from your Google Drive settings.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.
  • Service Providers: To third-party services listed above, only to the extent necessary for app functionality.
  • Business Transfers: If Pocket Pal is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred (you will be notified).

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time:
    • Local data: Uninstall the app or use "Clear Data" in Settings.
    • Cloud backup: Go to Google Drive → Settings → Manage Apps → Pocket Pal → Delete hidden app data.
    • Account: Sign out and delete your local data.
  • Portability: You can export your data as a JSON file from the app's Settings.
  • Opt-out of Ads: Purchase Pocket Pal Premium to remove all ads.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke app permissions in your device settings (notifications, exact alarms, etc.).

8. Children's Privacy
