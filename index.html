About PocketPal

PocketPal is an Android budgeting and expense-tracking app developed and maintained by Bewandera Labs. It helps people monitor spending, manage budgets, set savings goals, and securely back up their financial data to their own Google Drive account. PocketPal is available worldwide on Google Play.

About Bewandera Labs

Bewandera Labs is an independent software developer based in Nairobi, Kenya, building privacy-first personal finance tools for Android. PocketPal is our flagship app. You can reach us anytime at bewanderalabs@gmail.com.

How it works

Four steps to a budget you'll keep

  1. Set your monthly budget — split it across the categories that matter to you.
  2. Log expenses as they happen — quick entry, receipt photo optional.
  3. Watch your ring fill in real time — always know exactly what's left to spend.
  4. Let it back up quietly — your encrypted data syncs to your own Google Drive, automatically.
Security

Your data belongs to you

We built PocketPal around a simple rule: your financial data should stay yours — not ours to see, store, or sell.

How your data is protected
  • Encrypted end-to-end (AES-256-GCM) before it ever leaves your device
  • Only you hold the passphrase — we never see it and can't recover it if it's lost
  • Backups are stored in your own personal Google Drive, never on our servers
  • We do not sell your personal information, ever
